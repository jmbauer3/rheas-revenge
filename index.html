Game Rules

  • Each player takes turns guessing where other players will place their pieces.
  • After two guesses, players place their own piece on the board.
  • If two or more players place their pieces in the same spot, no one claims it.
  • At the end of each round, all pieces and guesses are revealed and scores are tallied.
  • Guesses are shown as outlines around squares in the player's color.
  • Placed pieces remain on the board until the end of the game.
  • Players can capture each other's squares by placing their piece on an occupied square.
  • The game continues until there are no more free squares.
